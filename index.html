October 27th & 28th, 2016

Pittsburgh, PA, USA

Doubletree Pittsburgh Downtown

Rust Belt Rust is a conference for people of any level of Rust experience-- you're welcome even if you're just interested in Rust!

  • Day 1 will be interactive workshops, including a few at an introductory level. Bring your laptop for hands-on learning in smaller groups.
  • Day 2 will be a single track of 30 minute talks on a variety of Rust related topics.

Why is the conference taking place in the Rust Belt? For the pun opportunities, of course!! We also enjoy the opportunity to show off our region-- there are more people doing interesting things with technology here than you might have guessed!

Rust Belt Rust is being organized by Carol (Nichols || Goulding), Jake Goulding, Alex Burkhart, Dylan McGuire, Andrew Hobden, Corey Farwell, and Bruce Adams, with help and advice from Steve Klabnik, Ashley Williams, and Vijay Korapaty.

Rust Belt Rust is not an official Rust or Mozilla event.

We are also not affiliated with the RustBelt project, though we do share a love of Rust-related puns :)
